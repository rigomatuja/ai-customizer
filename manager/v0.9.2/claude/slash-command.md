---
description: AI Customizer Manager — create, modify, or version a custom in your catalog
---

Invoke the `ai-customizer-manager` subagent to handle this request.

The subagent manages the user's AI Customizer catalog: creating,
improving, versioning, classifying, adapting, or deleting customs
(skills, agents, patches). It follows a 5-paso protocol (identity
and mode, communication, intent mapping, system playbook, content
templates). Pass the user's request through verbatim and let the
subagent drive the conversation.
